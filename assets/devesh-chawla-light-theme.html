Biography

The Architect of India's Startup Revolution

Devesh Chawla is an ingenious strategist, mentor, effective leader, out-of-the-box thinker, trader, and all-rounder. Known and highly respected for his brisk planning and execution, he is the Founder and CEO of Chatur Ideas and Chatur Wealth, two ventures built on the shared conviction that India's youth deserves the tools and support to become financially self-reliant.

"My mission is to build an ecosystem where every entrepreneur — from a college student to a seasoned founder — has access to the funding, guidance, and network they need to scale."

Chatur Ideas is his heartfelt and sincere effort to create a one-stop solution for startups and entrepreneurs. It is one of India's largest startup enabling platforms, with over 1,100 startups and access to 1,500+ investors, offering startups a place to get funding, mentoring, and 360° execution support. After acquiring Nurture Talent Academy — India's first Institute for Entrepreneurs, present across 125+ cities and having trained more than 34,000 entrepreneurs — Chatur Ideas is embedding entrepreneurship culture at the grassroots level.

Beyond Chatur Ideas, Devesh founded Chatur Wealth, a fintech startup built on rigorously tested algo-trading strategies centred around Nifty Options, with an objective to provide superior returns compared to Nifty benchmarks and equity mutual funds. He is deeply committed to helping India's youth achieve financial independence, whether through entrepreneurship, employment, or the stock market.

He has been invited as Guest of Honour, Speaker, Panelist, and Jury at major startup events across India and globally. He has been covered by Forbes India, various national newspapers, TV channels, and international business media.

Vision & Philosophy

A Leader Who Believes in Building
Others Up

Devesh Chawla's leadership philosophy is anchored in empowerment, honesty and the conviction that India's entrepreneurial potential is vastly untapped.

"India is changing — and the entrepreneurs we mentor today are the builders of tomorrow's economy. My job is to remove every barrier between a great idea and the funding and guidance it needs to become a great company."

— Devesh Chawla, Founder & CEO, Chatur Ideas
1
Empowerment Over Dependency

True mentoring means building founders who can lead independently — not creating dependency on the mentor. Devesh's programs are built to transfer skills, mindset and networks.

2
Execution is Everything

Ideas are the beginning, not the result. Chatur Ideas' entire model is built on 360° execution support because a great idea without execution is worthless.

3
Access Democratises Success

The best entrepreneurs don't always come from the top cities or elite colleges. By reaching 125+ cities and 750+ colleges, Devesh ensures that geography and pedigree don't determine destiny.

4
Financial Independence as a Right

Through both Chatur Ideas and Chatur Wealth, Devesh is committed to helping India's youth achieve financial independence — through entrepreneurship, careers, or intelligent investing.

Devesh Chawla is the Founder and CEO of Chatur Ideas — India's largest startup enabling platform — and Chatur Wealth, a fintech algo-trading venture. He is a Forbes Top 100 Great People Managers awardee (2021), Mahatma Gandhi Samman recipient, 2x TEDx Speaker, and Entrepreneur of the Year 2018. He has personally mentored 1,100+ startups, built a 1,500+ investor network, and trained 34,000+ entrepreneurs across India, making him one of the country's most respected startup mentors and ecosystem builders.

Chatur Ideas is India's largest startup enabling platform, founded by Devesh Chawla in 2015. It provides 360° execution support to startups across four pillars: Fundraising (access to 1,500+ investors), Mentoring (50+ expert mentors from Google, BNP Paribas, Silicon Valley etc.), Entrepreneurship Development Programs (across 750+ colleges), and Outsourced Innovation (go-to-market, legal, sales team building). After acquiring Nurture Talent Academy, it also runs entrepreneurship programs in 125+ cities.

Startups can reach out to Chatur Ideas via info@chaturideas.com or contact Devesh Chawla directly at deveshchawla@chaturideas.com or +91-9820765679. Chatur Ideas works with curated startups only, focusing on quality over quantity. The team will evaluate the venture, discuss goals and determine the right support track — whether fundraising, mentoring, EDP, or outsourced innovation.

Chatur Wealth is a fintech startup also founded by Devesh Chawla, focused on growing investors' capital through rigorously tested algo-trading strategies on Nifty Options. While Chatur Ideas serves the startup ecosystem, Chatur Wealth is designed for retail and professional investors looking to achieve higher returns than Nifty benchmarks and equity mutual funds through systematic, algorithm-driven trading — democratising financial independence through the stock market.

Devesh's philosophy is rooted in four core beliefs: Empowerment over dependency (build independent founders, not reliant ones), Execution above all (ideas only matter when executed), Access democratises success (reaching tier-2 and tier-3 cities to ensure geography doesn't determine entrepreneurial destiny), and Financial independence as a right (every Indian deserves the knowledge and tools to build wealth). He believes the best mentor is one who makes themselves unnecessary over time.

In 2016, Chatur Ideas acquired Nurture Talent Academy, which was founded by Mr. Amit Grover (IIT-Delhi, IIM-Indore alumnus) in January 2010 and is India's 1st Institute for Entrepreneurs. The acquisition made Chatur Ideas one step closer to becoming India's leading entrepreneurship training company, extending its reach right to the college level.
